Key Features

  • Timeline Hierarchy: Nested timelines with independent time scales.
  • Reusable Clips: Define animations once (e.g., "Orbit", "Pulse") and apply to any object.
  • Typed Channels: Animate numbers, booleans, strings, and colors.
  • Convenience API: element.animate() with Promises.
  • Conflict Resolution: Auto-cancels overlapping animations.
  • Playback Control: Pause, resume, loop, yoyo, and speed control.
