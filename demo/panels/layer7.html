Layer 7 — Scene

A single Scene rendered four times, each showing a different quadrant of the scene bounds. Scroll to pan, Ctrl/⌘+scroll to zoom towards the cursor, and Shift+drag to pan — just like a CAD app.

Scene Demo

Each panel shows one quadrant of the scene. Scroll on a panel to zoom into it; Shift+drag or middle-click drag to pan. Each quadrant zooms and pans independently. Click any shape to randomise its colours; hover to see a dotted outline.

Scroll = pan · Ctrl/⌘+scroll = zoom · Shift+drag = pan · Click shape = random colour · Hover = dotted outline

Scene Size: 400×300
Quadrants: 4
FPS: —

Features Demonstrated

  • Quad-view compositing: Four source→dest mappings from the scene's quadrants
  • Ticker-driven: The Scene's Ticker drives rendering of all four mappings
  • Shared scene graph: Animated elements appear in whichever quadrant they overlap
  • Scroll pan: Scroll/trackpad to pan the view
  • Ctrl/⌘ zoom: Hold Ctrl or ⌘ and scroll to zoom toward the cursor
  • Shift+drag pan: Shift+left-click drag or middle-click drag to pan
  • Click interaction: Click any shape to randomise its gradient colours
  • Hover interaction: Hover over a shape to see a 3px white dotted stroke
  • Adjustable zoom: Each quadrant zoomed in to show detail
