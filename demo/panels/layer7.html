Layer 7 — Quad View

A single Scene rendered four times, each showing a different quadrant of the scene bounds at an adjustable zoom. The Scene's Ticker drives all four source→dest mappings every frame.

Quad View Demo

Each panel zooms into one quadrant of the scene. Animated elements move in the shared scene and appear in whichever quadrant they cross.

Scene Size: 400×300
Quadrants: 4
FPS: —

Features Demonstrated

  • Quad-view compositing: Four source→dest mappings from the scene's quadrants
  • Ticker-driven: The Scene's Ticker drives rendering of all four mappings
  • Shared scene graph: Animated elements appear in whichever quadrant they overlap
  • Adjustable zoom: Each quadrant zoomed in to show detail
