Layer 7 — Scene

A single Scene rendered four times, each showing a different quadrant of the scene bounds at an adjustable zoom. Mouse wheel zooms into the scene towards the cursor, and middle-click drags to pan — just like a CAD app.

Scene Demo

Each panel shows one quadrant of the scene. Scroll on a panel to zoom into it; middle-click drag to pan. Each quadrant zooms and pans independently.

Scene Size: 400×300
Quadrants: 4
FPS: —

Features Demonstrated

  • Quad-view compositing: Four source→dest mappings from the scene's quadrants
  • Ticker-driven: The Scene's Ticker drives rendering of all four mappings
  • Shared scene graph: Animated elements appear in whichever quadrant they overlap
  • Mouse wheel zoom: Scroll to zoom toward the cursor position
  • Middle-click pan: Hold middle mouse button and drag to pan the view
  • Adjustable zoom: Each quadrant zoomed in to show detail
